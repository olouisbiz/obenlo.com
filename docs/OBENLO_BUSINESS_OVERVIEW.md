## 🌎 Vision & Mission

### Vision
To become the definitive economic engine of the Caribbean service and hospitality industry, providing every local entrepreneur with the tools to compete on a global stage.

### Mission
Obenlo empowers service providers and hosts by providing a premium, trust-based marketplace integrated with a powerful business operating system. We bridge the gap between Caribbean talent and international demand through technology, transparency, and high-end design.

## 🏗 Business Model: The Service Marketplace

Obenlo operates as a **Commission-Based Marketplace** (Marketplace-as-a-Service). We monetize the platform through:

1.  **Transaction Commissions**: A percentage of every successful booking processed through the platform.
2.  **Platform Fees**: Service fees charged to guests to maintain the secure booking infrastructure and global payment gateways.
3.  **Tiered Verification (Future)**: Premium verification levels for "Elite" hosts to gain maximum exposure.

---

## 💎 The "Trust-First" Core (Operations)

What sets Obenlo apart from legacy booking scripts is our **"Path to Payout"** protocol, designed to solve the specific trust challenges in regional markets:

-   **Escrow Security**: Payments are captured upon booking but held securely by Obenlo.
-   **Check-In Verification**: Real-time attendance tracking to prevent "no-show" fraud.
-   **Payout-on-Completion**: Funds are only released to a host's withdrawable balance once they mark a service as **Completed**. This ensures platform integrity and significantly reduces chargeback risks.

## 📂 Product Architecture: The Business Hub

Obenlo is more than a booking site; it is a **Business Operating System** for hosts. We use a unique hierarchical logic:

### 1. Main Business Listing (The Identity)
The "Parent" listing represents the host's brand (e.g., a Boutique Hotel, a Tour Agency, or a Spa). It handles overarching trust signals: Verified ID, Global Reviews, and Location.

### 2. Bookable Units & Services (The Revenue)
Linked to the Main Listing are specific "Bookables" (e.g., individual rooms, specific tour dates, or service slots). This allows providers to manage complex inventories from a single, unified dashboard.

## 👥 User Ecosystem

Local and international travelers seeking high-quality Caribbean experiences. They benefit from a unified UI/UX, secure payments (Stripe/PayPal), and a transparent review system.

### The Providers (Supply)
Small to medium-sized Caribbean businesses. Obenlo provides them with a ready-made digital infrastructure:
-   **PWA Mobile App**: Full business management on the go.
-   **Support Ecosystem**: Direct line to platform administrators via the Help Center.
-   **Automated Marketing**: SEO-optimized listings and Social Media auto-posting.

## ⚙️ Technical Integrity

Obenlo is built on a custom-engineered WordPress core, decoupled into specialized modules for maximum uptime and horizontal scaling:

-   **`obenlo-booking`**: The proprietary transaction and availability engine.
-   **`obenlo-pwa`**: Delivering a native-app feel for mobile-first users.
-   **`obenlo-i18n`**: Advanced multilingual support for the diverse Caribbean market.
-   **`obenlo-security`**: Integrated VAPID-key encryption and secure WebHooks.
